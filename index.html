Our Vision:

Originally inspired by ArtFinder, we really appreciate the idea of being able to quickly find independent artists and their work, how to support each artist, and add ways that you can recreate your own version of art without confusion.

Our Audience:

The audience that we hope to reach includes those that are exploring different ways to craft, looking for mentors, or just looking for a gift for the holidays!

Our goals and what we do:

Our main goal is simple: exposure exposure exposure!

We can't wait to show you the artists we've partnered with and possibly work with you some day! Here is a safe place to explore art, practice craft, ask questions, and support independent artists with their small businesses.
